About ACRC

Aging Care and Rehabilitation Center (ACRC) provides homecare services onsite and offsite that are responsive to individual needs and promote health and wellbeing.

Our comprehensive programs and services range from assessment, treatment, to family counselling group, support and is provided by a broad network of professionals.

ACRC ensure to provide excellent short term and long term care for the individuals that require healthcare at home and at ACRC center. ACRC provides the regular updates to family members who resides in Nepal as well abroad about the health and well-being of their dear ones.

Services

ACRC's services are designed specifically for your medical needs/cares, preferences and budget. ACRC offers wide range of adult services from hours a visit to 24 hours a day offsite and 24 hours of service onsite. Following are the services ACRC provides offsite and onsite services:

  1. We help and provide services to adults with different health issues that includes early dementia, aging care, physiotherapy, nutritionist, dietician, referrals services. personal care, nursing services etc.
  2. Prescribed medicines, laboratory test, blood test, stool test, insulin test, pregnancy test etc, sample collection and delivery.
  3. Provide home delivery services for medications and other medical facilities as per to request and requirement.
  4. Provide workshops for individuals, families and caregivers as well extensive education and consultation services.
  5. Provide complete medical examination by highly qualified and experienced medical professionals in every six months or whenever required,
  6. Provide Re-creational Center onsite where individuals can spend their leisure time, engage themselves. The Re-creational Center will provide onsite and offsite programs and activities.

About Us

Aging Care and Rehabilitation Center (ACRC) provides homecare services onsite and offsite that are responsive to individual needs and promote health and wellbeing.

Our comprehensive programs and services range from assessment, treatment, to family counselling group, support and is provided by a broad network of professionals.

ACRC ensure to provide excellent short term and long term care for the individuals that require healthcare at home and at ACRC center. ACRC provides the regular updates to family members who resides in Nepal as well abroad about the health and well-being of their dear ones.


Activities

Facilities

ACRC offers many onsite and offsite services to help meet an individual’s need. ACRC provides a variety of medical services for adults with different health issues. In addition, ACRC offers an Information Resource Center (IRS) and a computer lab for individuals to be actively engaged in the program.

Outdoor Activities

ACRC provides many outdoor activities to allow individuals to be actively engaged offsite of the facility. Adults are encouraged to attend picnics, as well as, offsite trips such as Holy Places and malls.

Indoor Activities

ACRC provides a variety of indoor services that allow individuals to be actively engaged in the program. In addition, ACRC offers a range of workshops for individuals. Individuals within the program are also offered entertainment services at times, such as movies as per request.
